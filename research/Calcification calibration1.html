Calcification calibration

Dual-energy mammography can suppress the contrast between adipose and glandular tissues and improve the detectability of microcalcifications (mCs). In the published papers, mCs were calibrated by aluminum and identified by their thickness. The variety of compositions of mCs brings about huge calculation errors.

Table 1 lists the percentage errors induced by aluminum calibration using the traditional method, the true mC thickness ranges from 100mm to 500mm, and correction coefficient is 0.7.
